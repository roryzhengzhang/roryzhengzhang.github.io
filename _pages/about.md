About Me
======
Zheng Zhang is MS student in computer science and junior HCI researcher at [GrouLens Research](https://grouplens.org/), [University of Minnesota](https://twin-cities.umn.edu/). His ressearch interest focuses on human-coumpter interaction, social computing, research through interface design and value-sensitive algorithm design. His MS advisor is [Haiyi Zhu](https://haiyizhu.com). His current research enthusiasm focues on UI design method reducing underlying discrimination in machine learning algorithm.
